How to Make Butter Paneer

Making butter paneer is easy! Just follow these steps:

  1. Heat milk in a pan until it boils.
  2. Remove from heat and add a squeeze of lemon juice.
  3. Let it sit for a few minutes, then strain the curds.
  4. Rinse the paneer under cold water and set aside.
  5. Heat butter in a pan and add the paneer.
  6. Sauté for a few minutes until golden brown.
